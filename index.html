Frequently Asked Questions

What is an employee well-being challenges platform?

An employee well-being challenges platform is the software a company uses to run wellness challenges, team challenges, and fitness competitions for its workforce. Goal AI is a modern take on that category: instead of giving every employee the same step-count goal, Goal AI personalizes the challenge to each person so more of the workforce actually participates.

How are Goal AI's team challenges different from legacy wellness challenges?

Legacy wellness challenges give everyone the same target. That works for the 20% of employees who love a leaderboard, and it loses the other 80%. Goal AI uses AI to shape a different challenge for every person. Runners run, new parents sleep, desk workers stand and stretch, and it all still rolls up to team and company scoreboards. The result is a wellness competition where everyone can compete, not just the already-fit.

Can Goal AI run a global corporate challenge across a distributed workforce?

Yes. Goal AI runs global corporate challenges across time zones, languages, and cultures. Coaching prompts localize, measurement units adapt, and challenge cadence respects regional working patterns so a global well-being challenge feels native to each office, not exported from headquarters.

What kinds of employee health challenges does Goal AI support?

Goal AI supports team challenges, individual challenges, company-wide wellness competitions, fitness competitions, step challenges, sleep challenges, mindfulness and breathwork challenges, nutrition challenges, strength training challenges, and multi-week global well-being challenges. Every challenge can be personalized at the individual level while feeding the same team and company scoreboards.

Which wearables and data sources does Goal AI integrate with?

Goal AI integrates with Apple Health, Oura, Whoop, Garmin, Fitbit, Strava, and Polar, along with calendar and daily mood check-ins. Employees bring the device they already use. No one has to switch ecosystems to join a corporate wellness challenge.

How does Goal AI protect employee health data?

Employee health data in Goal AI is encrypted in transit and at rest. Employers see aggregated, de-identified participation and engagement metrics. Individual health data stays between the employee and their AI coach. The platform follows HIPAA-aligned practices for covered populations.
